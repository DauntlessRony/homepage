Hi!

My name is Nafees Bin Zafar. I develop techniques and software for the visual effects industry. I am originally from Dhaka, Bangladesh, but I usually call Los Angeles "home".

I am the Director of Research and Development at Oriental DreamWorks in Shanghai. I have worked on many films over the last 13 years such as Pirates of the Caribbean: At World's End, 2012, and Kung Fu Panda 3. In 2008 I won an Academy Award for my work on pioneering digital fluid effects now used throughout the visual effects industry. In 2015 I received an Academy Award for the development of the Drop large-scale destruction simulation system.

I believe in sharing research, and building strong ties between academia and industry. I have served on juries for SIGGRAPH, and have been a Program Chair for the Digital Production Symposium. I have also served as a member of the Digital Imaging Technology Subcommittee of the Academy of Motion Picture Arts and Sciences, which investigates groundbreaking technical contributions to the film industry for Academy Awards.
